Hotdog Roll-Ups

Description

Just when you thought hot dogs couldn’t get more fun, here's
a tasty new twist. These bite-sized snacks are not only easy
to make, but they’re also loaded with all your favorite hot dog
fixin’s: Cheddar cheese, tangy mustard, crunchy onions, and a
touch of sweet pickle relish. Paired with the creamy mayo dip,
they’re irresistible for kids and adults alike.
