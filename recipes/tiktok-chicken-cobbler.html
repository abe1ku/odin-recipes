TikTok Chicken Cobbler

Description

This scrumptious chicken cobbler brings together the best of
comfort food in one simple dish—a creamy shredded chicken
base topped with golden-brown, buttery biscuit topping. After
it became a viral sensation delighting TikTok audiences
everywhere, we had to try the original recipe by
@yourbarefootneighbor. The one-pan ease of layering melted
butter, shredded rotisserie chicken, frozen vegetables, creamy
soup, milk, and boxed Red Lobster Cheddar Bay biscuit mix
makes it a weeknight hero. Whether you’re after a quick-to-
prep family dinner or a cozy potluck dish to feed a hungry
crowd, this recipe ticks all the boxes!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 cups rotisserie chicken meat, shredded
  • 1 (15 ounce) bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 (11.35 ounce) package Cheaddar bay biscuit mix
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 375°F
    (190°C). Place butter in the bottom of a 9x13-inch
    baking dish and place in the oven to melt.
  2. When butter is melted, add chicken in a single layer
    over the buter. Top evenly with frozen veggies.
  3. Stir together biscuit mix, seasoning packet from the
    mix, and milk in a bowl until well blended; pour over
    the chicken and veggies. Spread lightly and evenly
    over the top of the mixture but do not stir.
  4. Whisk together soup and stock in a second bowl until
    smooth. Pour over biscuit mix but do not stir.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven uncovered until very golden
    brown and only very slightly jiggly in the center, about
    45 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle parsley over the top if desired and let stand
    for 10 minutes before serving.
